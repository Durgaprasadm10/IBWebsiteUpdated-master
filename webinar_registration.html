CYBER SECURITY WEBINAR

Security and Privacy concerns in the context of the adoption of mandated mobile apps such as the COVID vax passport

In this webinar, we will present the security and privacy concerns of the mobile apps that require disclosure of personal data, which are highly targeted by malicious actors.

Session: September 22nd 2021 at 11 AM (Eastern Time)

The webinar will cover:

  • Security vulnerabilities of apps that collect personal data of the users
  • How users can protect themselves while using the apps
  • How developers should code to protect the users' data

Speaker:
Thierry Bela

Chief Architect - Security & Performance manager

At Ideabytes Inc, with over 15 years of experience under his belt Thierry is responsible for securing applications against vulnerabilities exposed to users with legal access. Guides development teams to structure code in compliance to the methodologies preventing cyber vulnerabilities. Mentors graduating cyber security engineers in ethical hacking.
